## Sunday Session

### Ni no Ashi Drills

- Forwards, front leg, then back leg
- Backwards with back leg then front leg
- Forwards with back leg then front leg
- Backwards with front leg then back leg
- Forwards, back leg crosses over front leg sideways, front leg steps forward
- Bacwards, front leg steps over back leg and then back leg steps back

### Hensoku Drill

#### Type I

- Hensoku step
  
  Hidari chudangamae, right step 45 degrees, followed by left leg and left leg to 45
  
- Ko-soku step

  To return. Be mindful of which hand is cutting

#### Type II

- Hensoku step

  First leg, do a half fukuteki. Second step do an untai punch.

- Ko-soku step
